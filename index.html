About the shop

Nikita Mhaisalkar is the creative force behind the luxe couture label “Nikita Mhaisalkar” that was launched in 2003. She arrived in the Indian high fashion industry as a self-trained designer and an MBA in Entrepreneurship. With the rising demand of her premium clientele, she started her formal training for an advanced degree in fashion design, alongside heading her unit.
